SECURITY MEASURES
------------------------------------------------------------------------
ISO/IEC 27001:2022-certified Information Security Management System;
biometric data stored only as irreversible, encrypted templates (never
raw fingerprint or facial images) per the "reasonable security
safeguards" required under Section 8(5) of the DPDP Act; AES-256
encryption at rest and TLS 1.3 in transit; CCTV footage stored on
encrypted, access-logged NVRs with role-based retrieval; Tier III/Tier
IV facility controls audited to Uptime Institute standards; quarterly
access-control reviews and mandatory multi-factor authentication for
all administrative systems.
