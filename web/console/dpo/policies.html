Validation Quick Tips

  • Language parity: Make sure all field names are filled-in all supported languages.
  • Unique Purpose ID: The "id" field in data_processing_purposes must be a unique string.
  • Referential Integrity: data_categories_involved must be a subset of data_categories_details.
  • Retention Value: retention_duration_value is integer.
  • Retention Units: retention_duration_unit must be YEARS / MONTHS / DAYS / HOURS / MINUTES.
  • Lifecycle Events: retention_start_event must be COLLECTION or CESSATION.
  • Data Types: Sharing lists must be arrays. Mandatory/Sensitive flags must be Boolean.
